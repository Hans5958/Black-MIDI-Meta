    Links

    Software

    Players

    • Piano From Above - Mostly used MIDI player on the community.
    • Synthesia - Mainstream piano roll software/MIDI player, often used by YouTube creators, paid.
    • Synthesia (9.0) - Latest free version of Synthesia.
    • Ultralight MIDI Player - Alternative to PFA, made with Java. Can be used for rendering MIDI files.
    • Zenith - MIDI renderer, made with C#, alternative to UMP.
    • Kiva - MIDI player, alternative to PFA.
    • mmidi - Lightweight MIDI player.
    • Toms's MIDI Player - Lightweight MIDI player.
    • PianoFall - Physics-based MIDI player.
    • MIDITrail - 3D MIDI player.
    • Embers - MIDI player from Unreal Engine. Alternative to Synthesia but looks professional.

    Synth/Driver

    • OmniMIDI - Most optimized for playing Black MIDI. Also works for general players.
    • VirtualMIDISynth - Was most used before OmniMIDI used, good alternative.

    Tools

    • Keppy's MIDI Converter - Convert MIDI files to MP3, WAV, or OGG.
    • Keppy's Note Generator - Render MIDITrail-like counter.
    • Simple AF Converter - Mostly used for merging MIDI files.
    • Simple AF Overlap Remover - Remove overlaps on MIDI files.
    • MidiNoteThreshold - Remove notes above the velocity threshold.
    • ImageToMidi - Converts image to a MIDI file. Good for making arts.
    • CJC Toolbox - Link to Discord server. A toolbox that includes CJC MIDI Counter Generator, CJC Overlap Remover, and CJC Advanced MIDI Merger. Seperate softwares are also in the Discord server.

    MIDI and Audio Editors

    • Domino - Free MIDI editor in Japanese. Use Domino English Translation or other translations.
    • FL Studio - Professional DAW, often used on the community.
    • Anvil Studio - Good for loading multi-channel tracks. Freemium.
    • Audacity - Basic audio editor. Open source.

    Video Editors

    • Shotcut - Free video editor.
    • DaVinci Resolve - Free and professional video editor.
    • Adobe Premiere Pro - Paid video editor.
    • Adobe After Effects - Paid video editor.
    • Vegas Pro - Paid video editor.

    Other Useful Links

    • Carlos S.M.'s Publico - Archive for all things Black MIDI by Carlos S.M.. Not frequently updated.
    • List of Largest MIDIs - List of largest MIDIs by note count.
    • Black MIDI Software Archive - My archive of softwares related to Black MIDI.
